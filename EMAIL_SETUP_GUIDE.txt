GMAIL APP PASSWORD SETUP GUIDE FOR ILLI
========================================

STEP 1: ENABLE 2-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION
----------------------------------------
1. Go to: https://myaccount.google.com/security
2. Sign in with your email: farhanhomeschooling519@gmail.com
3. Look for "2-Step Verification" section
4. Click on "2-Step Verification" 
5. Turn ON 2-factor authentication
6. Follow the setup process (phone number or authenticator app)

STEP 2: GENERATE APP PASSWORD
----------------------------------
1. After 2FA is enabled, go to: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
2. Sign in with your email: farhanhomeschooling519@gmail.com
3. Under "Select app", choose: "Other (Custom name)"
4. Under "Select device", choose: "Windows Computer" or "Other"
5. In the name field, type: ILLI Virtual Assistant
6. Click "GENERATE"
7. Copy the 16-character password (it will look like: xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx)
8. Save this password securely

STEP 3: UPDATE ILLI SCRIPT
------------------------------
1. Open the ILLI script: g:/Virtual Assistant/illi_final.py
2. Find line around 35 that says:
   sender_password = "your-app-password"
3. Replace "your-app-password" with your generated app password
4. Save the file

STEP 4: TEST EMAIL FUNCTIONALITY
----------------------------------
1. Start ILLI: Double-click "START_ILLI_FINAL.bat"
2. Say: "send email to test@gmail.com subject Test message This is a test email from ILLI"
3. ILLI should send the email successfully

IMPORTANT NOTES:
--------------
- The app password is NOT your regular Gmail password
- Keep the app password secure and private
- You can generate multiple app passwords for different apps
- If you lose the app password, generate a new one
- 2FA must be enabled to generate app passwords

DIRECT LINKS:
-------------
Google Account Security: https://myaccount.google.com/security
App Passwords Page: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
2FA Setup: https://myaccount.google.com/security/2stepverification

TROUBLESHOOTING:
---------------
- If email doesn't send, check:
  * 2FA is enabled
  * App password is correct (no spaces)
  * Less secure apps access is turned ON
  * Gmail account is not blocked

- Common errors:
  * "Application-specific password required" -> Enable 2FA
  * "Invalid credentials" -> Check app password spelling
  * "SMTP error" -> Check internet connection
